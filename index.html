What is this system ?

Fraud detection is a must in the modern world. With the exponential growth of the economy, frauds have become a major problem, especially in the banking industry. Most of the time, these frauds are detected by bank officers. We are automating the fraud detection processs with Artifical Interligences.

Fraud Detection Based on Human Facial Expressions

Humans can identify or inspect, whether a user is trying to do a fraudulent activity or trying to lie by looking at the person. On average people don’t do much better than a coin flip. But by using machine learning techniques we can automate and increase the accuracy of the detection of fraudulent activities.

Fraud Detection Based on Human Speaking Patterns

Human can identify frauds, lies, deceits or scams by another person’s voice or speaking pattern. Using Deep Learning techiniques we can identify frauds by analyzing voice signals.

Fraud Detection Based on Human Behavioural Patterns

Since the past humans have been able to identify fraudulent activities by looking at another person's body-language and gestures, there are highly paid individuals whose job is to find out those abnormal behavioral patterns. So, In this system we have automated this process with deep learning techniques.

Fraud Detection Based on Transactions Patterns

In this component, customer’s transaction history is checked for unusual patterns. we need to identify a wide array of fraud scenarios to predict a fraud activity using transaction details.
